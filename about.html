About Defining Technology.

We have a simple goal here - make quality software, accessible.

If you've got a problem you think software could help solve, we're here to help guide you through the process. No jargon, no buzzwords... Our aim is to make the process as simple as possible. At the end of the process, we deliver solutions that are high quality and flexible enough to be added to in the future. As your problem changes, the solution can too.

We're based at RookieOven, a fantastic coworking space at the Fairfield Shipyard Offices in Glasgow. Some of the greatest technical innovations of the Glasgow shipbuilding era came out of these offices. Now, startups work side by side to keep that tradition of innovation alive.
